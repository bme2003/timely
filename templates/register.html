How to Get Your Canvas Calendar Link

To add your Canvas calendar link:

  1. Log into Canvas.
  2. Go to the "Calendar" section in the global navigation on the left.
  3. Click on "Calendar Feed" (usually located at the bottom right).
  4. Copy the provided URL.
  5. Paste it here in the "Canvas iCal URL" field to sync your Canvas calendar with Timely.
